Place

Work To Protect These Places.We need to prioritize the protection of places based on their wealth and variety of life, the challenges they face, and our ability to make a positive impact on them.

Conserving wildlife and natural resources is essential across all continents, from the Arctic to the Eastern Himalayas. By partnering with individuals and organizations both locally and globally, and supporting inclusive conservation efforts in ecologically significant regions, we can halt and reverse the decline in wildlife numbers and unsustainable use of resources. To protect tigers, we must collaborate with the governments of the 13 nations that harbor wild tigers, committing to doubling tiger populations through conservation efforts in exceptional habitats to promote harmony between people and nature.
