Learn Some Vocabulary

Progress: The term "progress" refers to a forward movement or advancement towards a desired goal, outcome, or improvement. It involves making positive changes, developments, or advancements in various aspects of life, such as society, technology, knowledge, or personal growth.

lethergy: The term "lethargy" refers to a state of extreme tiredness, lack of energy, or a general feeling of sluggishness and apathy. It is often characterized by a lack of motivation or interest in engaging in activities, both physically and mentally. Lethargy can manifest as a sense of drowsiness, fatigue, or a persistent feeling of being drained or unenergetic.

Onset: The term "onset" refers to the beginning or initial occurrence of something, typically a condition, symptom, event, or process. It represents the point in time when a specific phenomenon starts or becomes noticeable.
